# Awesome Embodied AI & Robotics [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of awesome frameworks, open-source hardware, foundational models, and resources for Embodied Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.

Embodied AI is the next frontier of artificial intelligence, where computational agents interact with the physical world. This repository aggregates the absolute state-of-the-art (SOTA) open-source ecosystems.

## Quadruped & Locomotion
*Open-source mechanical designs, control codebases, and ecosystems for robot dogs and bipedal robots.*

* [OpenDogeRobotics](https://github.com/OpenDogeRobotics) - A fully open-source quadruped robot ecosystem, heavily optimized for high-performance edge computing.
* [MIT Cheetah Software](https://github.com/mit-biomimetics/Cheetah-Software) - The legendary open-source control code for the MIT Cheetah robot, a baseline for modern quadruped locomotion.
* [Stanford Quadruped](https://github.com/stanfordroboticsclub/StanfordQuadruped) - A highly accessible, low-cost open-source quadruped robot project.
* [CyberDog_ROS2](https://github.com/MiRoboticsLab/cyberdog_ros2) - Xiaomi's open-source ROS 2 codebase for their commercial CyberDog platform.

## Manipulation & Robotic Arms
*Projects focused on grasping, mobile manipulation, and fine motor control.*

* [ALOHA (A Low-cost Open-source Hardware System for Bimanual Teleoperation)](https://github.com/tonyzhaozh/aloha) - Stanford's viral open-source bimanual teleoperation system for imitation learning.
* [MoveIt](https://moveit.ros.org/) - The industry-standard motion planning framework for robotic arms, deeply integrated with ROS.
* [Franka ROS](https://github.com/frankaemika/franka_ros) - Official ROS integration for the Franka Emika Panda, the standard arm for AI research.

## Embodied Foundation Models
*Large AI models designed specifically to output physical actions, not just text or images.*

* [LeRobot](https://github.com/huggingface/lerobot) - Hugging Face's brand-new initiative providing state-of-the-art machine learning models for real-world robotics.
* [Octo Model](https://github.com/octo-models/octo) - An open-source, versatile foundation model for robotic manipulation.
* [Google RT-1/RT-2 (Papers & Code)](https://robotics-transformer2.github.io/) - Google DeepMind's groundbreaking Robotics Transformer models.

## Simulators & Sim-to-Real
*Virtual physics environments for training embodied agents using Reinforcement Learning (RL) before real-world deployment.*

* [Isaac Gym / Isaac Sim](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac-gym) - NVIDIA's ultra-high-performance physics simulation environments utilizing GPU acceleration.
* [MuJoCo](https://github.com/google-deepmind/mujoco) - DeepMind's free, open-source, and highly accurate physics engine for robotics research.
* [Habitat](https://github.com/facebookresearch/habitat-lab) - Meta's open-source 3D simulator for training embodied AI in photorealistic indoor environments.

## Hardware & Edge Compute
*The physical brains and nervous systems powering embodied agents.*

* [Orange Pi](http://www.orangepi.org/) - High-performance open-source single-board computers (like the Orange Pi 5), delivering exceptional TOPS for edge AI computation in robotics.
* [NVIDIA Jetson](https://developer.nvidia.com/embedded/jetson-modules) - The industry-standard GPU-accelerated computing platforms for autonomous machines.
* [ROS 2 (Robot Operating System)](https://docs.ros.org/en/rolling/) - The definitive open-source middleware suite for robot software development.
