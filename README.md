# Awesome Embodied AI & Robotics [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of awesome frameworks, open-source hardware, algorithms, and resources for Embodied Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.

Embodied AI is the next frontier of artificial intelligence, where agents interact with the physical world. This list aims to aggregate the most high-quality, state-of-the-art (SOTA) open-source projects in this domain.

## Quadruped Robots
*Open-source mechanical designs, codebases, and ecosystems for robot dogs.*

* [OpenDogeRobotics](https://github.com/OpenDogeRobotics) - A fully open-source quadruped robot ecosystem, optimized for high performance using accessible hardware like the Orange Pi 5.
* [Mini Pupper](https://github.com/mangdangroboticsclub/QuadrupedRobot) - An open-source, ROS-based robot dog for education and research.
* [Stanford Quadruped](https://github.com/stanfordroboticsclub/StanfordQuadruped) - The classic open-source quadruped robot project.

## Simulators & Environments
*Virtual environments for training embodied agents using reinforcement learning.*

* [Isaac Gym](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac-gym) - NVIDIA's high-performance physics simulation environment.
* [MuJoCo](https://mujoco.org/) - A free and open-source physics engine, highly accurate for robotics simulation.

## Hardware & Boards
*The compute brains powering embodied agents.*

* [Orange Pi](http://www.orangepi.org/) - High-performance open-source single-board computers, excellent for edge AI computation.
* [NVIDIA Jetson](https://developer.nvidia.com/embedded/jetson-modules) - Industry standard edge AI computing platforms.
